THE MXD: Your answer to telephone service compatibility

The MXD is the first traditional PERS on the market to be completely VoIP compatible. When a customer switches from POTS to VoIP, the MXD simply makes the call when needed. As more people get rid of their POTS lines and switch to the other mediums of communication, the MXD will continue to become more and more valuable to PERS dealers.
